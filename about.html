About & Safety

Engineering-first aerial services with a focus on safety and data integrity.

Who we are

I am a Computer Engineering student at the University of West Florida, building professional drone services under the EAZ Drones brand. Our approach combines engineering-level rigor with cinematic production workflows to deliver reliable, high-quality aerial media and technical data.

Safety & Certifications

  • Part 107 Certified remote pilot
  • Fully insured for commercial operations
  • Engineering-first flight planning and data validation

Operational Standards

We prioritize safety: pre-flight checklists, risk assessments, and compliance with FAA regulations are mandatory for every mission. Data integrity is enforced through calibrated sensors, redundancies, and documented workflows for capture and post-processing.
